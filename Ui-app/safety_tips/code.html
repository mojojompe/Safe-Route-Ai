Be Aware of Your Surroundings

Avoid distractions like your phone or headphones. Pay attention to people and vehicles around you.

Stick to Well-Lit Areas

When walking at night, choose routes that are well-illuminated and populated. Avoid shortcuts through dark alleys.

Secure Your Vehicle

Always lock your car doors and keep valuables out of sight. Park in secure, well-lit parking areas whenever possible.

Trust Your Instincts

If a situation or person makes you feel uneasy, leave the area immediately. Your intuition is a powerful safety tool.

Maintain Safe Following Distance

Keep a safe distance from the car in front of you to allow for sudden stops. The "three-second rule" is a good guideline.
